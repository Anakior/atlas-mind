# The Zettelkasten idea

A *Zettelkasten* ("slip box") is a method, not an app: you write small, atomic
notes — one idea each — and you **link** them densely. Over years the links, not
the folders, become the real value. Sound familiar? It is the same intuition
behind [[features/the-mind-graph|the mind graph]].

## Three rules that travel well

1. **One idea per note.** Small notes recombine; big notes rot.
2. **Link generously.** A note with no links is a note you'll never find again.
3. **Let structure emerge.** Don't pre-build a taxonomy — let clusters appear
   from the links you actually make.

## Why it lives in a node

This note is published from another mind and *mirrored* here read-only. That is
the point of a node: a good idea can be **followed**, not copy-pasted — when Mira
updates it, every mind that subscribes sees the new version. Back to the
overview: [[remotes/mira-garden/start-here|Mira's public garden]].
